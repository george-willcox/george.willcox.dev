Hello there! I'm George Willcox, this is just a place to share a small selection of the many things that I have created. I'm currently employed as a Software Engineer at Rocket Software, where I'm on the JCL team. There I work pretty much exclusively with COBOL, and other tools relating to the mainframe. But also write and maintain scripts written in languages such as Perl, Bash, and Shell Script.

I graduated from the University of Cambridge in 2023 with a degree in Computer Science, my college was Trinity Hall.

My main love is programming; this is something that I spend much of my free time doing. For my personal projects, I mostly code using Python or Javascript, though I have experience using many other languages too. Please take a look in the projects page to see some of the things I've worked on.

I'm also quite into model building and miniature painting at the moment, especially Warhammer 40k. I get a great deal of enjoyment out of painting, and I find it very relaxing and cathartic. But also really quite enjoy playing the tabletop game, and have started going to competetive events for it.

My other interests include: Hiking, travelling, Lego Star Wars, Magic the Gathering, bridge, and kayaking. See more details on the interests page.
